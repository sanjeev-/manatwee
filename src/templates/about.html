i have two primary goals with this website. foremost is to make a simple, clutter-free, and completely independent site for film & television sentiment aggregation & analysis

the motivation behind this was the growing importance of rotten tomatoes and metacritic as review sites. rt is owned by comcast and time warner, and metacritic is owned by cbs. they are putatively independent but i dont think it would hurt to have an independent site.

the second motivation for this website is to create and train a crowd-trained RNN (recurrent neural network) for sentiment analysis. current popular models are based off IMDB databases and this RNN has the ability to grow vastly larger thanks to crowd-sourced input.

if you would like access to the RNN weights and the training set, please dont hesitate to email me at manatwee at gmail dot com. i am also more than happy to share bag of words features.

simultaneously, im looking to build a collaborative filtering model using user data from reddit and twitter, to gain insight into real time pop culture tastes

the backend is in django, front end is bootstrap, and the deep learning is done via keras and tensorflow
